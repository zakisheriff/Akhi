## 🌟 Mission

**Akhi AI** (Arabic: أخي, meaning "My Brother") is your dedicated companion for authentic Islamic knowledge. In an age of information overload, we serve as a bridge to verified truth, prioritizing:

-   **Authenticity**: Zero tolerance for unverified information.
-   **Transparency**: Every answer is backed by clear references to primary sources.
-   **Inclusivity**: Respectful handling of differing valid scholarly opinions (Madhhabs).
-   **Adab (Etiquette)**: A user experience designed with the dignity of the content in mind.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 📚 Authentic Knowledge Engine
Powered by advanced artificial intelligence tuned for Islamic sciences, providing answers deeply rooted in:
-   **The Holy Quran** (with Tafsir context)
-   **Hadith Collections** (Al-Kutub Al-Sittah)
-   **Fiqh Encyclopedias**

### 🔬 AI Laboratory
Cutting-edge AI tools designed for modern utility:
-   **Halal Lens**: Instantly identify halal status of products by scanning ingredients lists with your camera.
-   **Quran Shazam**: Audio identification technology to name any Surah and Ayah from a live recitation.

### 🕊️ Spiritual Tools
-   **Tawbah Mode**: A guided multi-step journey for reflection, repentance, and hope.
-   **Smart Tasbih**: A digital counter with haptic feedback and "Dhikr Prescriptions" tailored to your mood.
-   **Shahada Witness**: A serene interface for those embracing or witnessing the declaration of faith.

### 📖 Knowledge Explorers
-   **Quran Reader**: High-performance, beautifully typeset digital Quran with multiple translations.
-   **Hadith Reader**: Daily gems from authentic collections to keep you grounded in the Sunnah.

### 🕋 Orientation & Calculation
-   **Qibla Finder**: Includes an immersive **AR Mode** and a high-precision 2D compass.
-   **Prayer Times**: Accurate local times with multiple calculation methods and Madhhab-specific settings.
-   **Mosque Finder**: Locate nearby Masjids with ease, including directions and details.
-   **Zakat Calculator**: Comprehensive tool to determine Zakat across all assets.
-   **Name Finder**: Library of beautiful Islamic names with deep linguistic meanings.

## 🕌 Madhhab-Aware Fiqh & Verified Citations

- **Strict Citation Policy**: Unlike standard AI, Akhi AI links every major claim to its source, allowing you to verify verses and hadiths directly.
- **Multiple Schools of Thought**: Tailor your learning experience by verifying rulings across the four major schools: **Hanafi, Shafi'i, Maliki, and Hanbali.**
